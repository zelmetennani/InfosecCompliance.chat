Frequently Asked Questions

Which compliance frameworks do you support?

We provide comprehensive support for all major compliance frameworks including SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS, NIST CSF, and more. Our experts can help you determine which frameworks are most relevant to your business.

How long does it typically take to achieve compliance?

The timeline varies based on your organization's size, complexity, and current security posture. SOC 2 Type I can typically be achieved in 3-6 months, while Type II requires an additional 6-12 months of control operation. Our team works to optimize this timeline while ensuring thorough implementation.

What makes your compliance services different?

Our partnership with Hacker Analytics combines AI-powered compliance guidance with hands-on expert implementation. This hybrid approach delivers more efficient, cost-effective compliance solutions that are tailored to your specific business needs.

Do you work with companies of all sizes?

Yes, we serve organizations ranging from startups to enterprise-level companies. Our services are scalable and can be customized to meet the needs and budgets of businesses at any stage of growth.

What is the cost of your compliance services?

Pricing depends on the scope of services, your organization's size, and the complexity of your environment. We offer flexible engagement models including project-based, retainer, and subscription options. Contact us for a customized quote.

Can you help maintain compliance after certification?

Absolutely. We offer ongoing compliance management services to help you maintain your certification, adapt to changing requirements, and continuously improve your security posture over time.
