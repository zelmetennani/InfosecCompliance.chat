Built by Compliance Professionals

Infosec Compliance Chat was created by the experts at Hacker Analytics who use it daily in their compliance practice.

About Hacker Analytics

Hacker Analytics is a team of infosec compliance professionals who specialize in helping organizations navigate complex regulatory requirements. With years of experience in the field, they've developed deep expertise in frameworks like SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, and more.

The team at Hacker Analytics built Infosec Compliance Chat as an internal tool to streamline their own compliance work. After seeing how effective it was at providing quick, accurate guidance, they decided to make it available to everyone.

Why We Created This Tool

We understand that compliance can be overwhelming, especially for teams without dedicated compliance resources. Our goal was to create a tool that makes compliance knowledge more accessible and helps organizations build stronger security programs without the usual complexity and confusion.

How We Use It

At Hacker Analytics, we use Infosec Compliance Chat daily to:

  • Quickly reference specific compliance requirements
  • Draft policy documents and implementation guides
  • Explain complex compliance concepts to clients
  • Stay updated on changing regulatory requirements

Need More Comprehensive Help?

While Infosec Compliance Chat is a powerful tool, we understand that some organizations prefer to work directly with compliance experts. If you're looking for hands-on assistance with your compliance program, the team at Hacker Analytics offers full-service compliance solutions.
