Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade from Free to Pro?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Your account settings and data will transfer seamlessly to your Pro account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and for Enterprise plans, we can arrange invoicing.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No, all plans are month-to-month or annual with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime.

What happens when I use all my free queries?

Once you've used your 10 free queries, you'll need to upgrade to the Pro plan to continue using the AI chat features.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team for details.

How secure is my data?

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry best practices for security and are ISO 27001 compliant ourselves.
